The mind behind the module

Mr. Ikraam Sadek is a Junior lecturer at Belgium Campus iTversity, Pretoria, where the mission and vision is to nurture every individual's excellence, "It's the way we're wired".

With a background of core development, AI systems, and project-based learning, Ikraam enforces real-world industry experience into every student and excels in practical demonstration and interactive methods. He means a lot to quite a number of students: He is highly favoured by upcoming IT specialists that are rady to save our planet. His concern is that most students rely on AI instead of actually understanding the concept.

When he's not debugging student code or architecting cloud solutions, you'll find him assisting and mentoring the hackathon team, brewing exceptionally strong coffee, or learning about the latest in generative AI.
